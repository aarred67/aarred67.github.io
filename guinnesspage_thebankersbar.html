The Bankers Bar

Located near Temple Bar in Dublin

The first bar during this five-day trip–The Bankers Bar! It was quite busy but had just a handful of empty bar stools, and it was perfect to sit and warm up after a cold, brisk day out in Dublin. The Guinness was quite good, and definitely better than most I’ve had in New York. It seemed like there were a few first dates going on around the pub which was quite sweet. It was an endearing little spot!

Scores:
  • Ambience: 3 / 5
  • Foam: 4 / 5
  • Taste: 4 / 5
  • Temperature: 5 / 5
  • Round Two? No, but was satisfied!
  • Overall: 4 / 5
