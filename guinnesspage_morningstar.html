The Morning Star

Located in the Cathedral Quarter in Belfast

We actually went to this pub for food! So no, we’re not just looking for our next pint wherever we go, but we did in fact have a pint here. I also really wanted to try steak and Guinness pie, and this place had a very delicious steak and Guinness pie. Loaded with gravy and mashed potatoes, the pie was perfectly done and had a bready crust that was incredible. The Guinness was not as memorable, but still quite good! I was satisfied.

Pictured above is my incredible food from that night. This is also where I watched my first few rounds of horse racing… I never realized how many silly names for horses there could be. I am also very bad at guessing which horse will win.

Scores:
  • Ambience: 3 / 5
  • Foam: 3 / 5
  • Taste: 4 / 5
  • Temperature: 5 / 5
  • Round Two? No, but there was Guinness in the steak pie!
  • Overall: 4 / 5
