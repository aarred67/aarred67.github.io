O'Neill's

Located near Temple Bar in Dublin

Located across from the famous Molly Malone statue, O’Neill’s was a festively packed two-floor pub full of recent Trinity College graduates and their families, all properly relaxed from a few pints—myself included. The live musician had a rasp to his voice that could easily be credited to his tendency to yell into the mic no matter the song, but he received applause after every song. Unlike The Bankers, O’Neill’s was not as intimate, but instead open and spacious for crowds to form and chatter loudly over one another.

Scores:

  • Foam: 4 / 5
  • Taste: 4 / 5
  • Temperature: 5 / 5
  • Round Two? Many more than that, yes.
